Our Activities

BOOKS & LIBRARY

Our library consists of useful and interesting stuff
for our pupils and their parents. We have the best materials to study, relax and play. There are also
multiple resources for kids.

GREAT TEACHERS

Our teachers are professionals and always thinking
about how to improve the learning process and engage
their pupils. They attend different educational seminars.

SPECIAL CERTIFICATION

Our pupils get a special Successful Primary School
Certification with their marks and achievements and
which lets to enter the secondary school without any exams.

GREAT FACILITIES

Our school has a new modern design. The classrooms
are equipped with all necessary stuff and devices; they
are big, spacious and light, with many plants.

FUNNY GAMES

Our educational process is based on a game method; we
provide our lessons with funny and active games. Kids
can study and relax at the same time.

DELICIOUS MEALS

Our canteen works during the lessons time. We provide
with a second breakfast and with lunch after the last
lesson. All meals consist of fresh and high-quality products.

VARIED CLASSES

We use an individual approach in studying, so kids study
all school subjects according to their interests and
talents.Our classes are diverse and innovational.

STUDENT'S ORCHESTRA

We have our own new school bus, which kids can use to
get to school. It drives at 8 o’clock in the morning and at
5 o’clock in the evening.

SUMMER PROGRAM

In the summertime kids can also spend their time with a
benefit. Smart KIDS has the Summer Camp, where our
teachers do summer activities with kids.

Strategies for Success

We are an academic residential college made up of students, scholars,
old collegians and staff members. Our rich history is the foundation for
our values.
